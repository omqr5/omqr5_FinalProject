# Metadata in the Digital World

Metadata is the **backbone** of the modern internet and data ecosystems.

## Where Metadata Powers Systems

### Search Engines
Use webpage metadata (title, description, tags) to rank search results.

### Websites & Accessibility
Image metadata helps screens readers interpret images. 

### Libraries & Databases
Use metadata to catalog, sort, and retrieve items instantly.

### Streaming & Shopping Platforms
Metadata is used for:
- Recommendations
- Categorization
- Personalization

It allows diital systems to connect different pieces of content intelligently.
