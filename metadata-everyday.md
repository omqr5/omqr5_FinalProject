# Metadata in Everyday Life

We use metadat constantly without realizing it.

## Photos
Metadata includes:
- Time & date taken
- GPS location
- Camera model
- Resolution & exsposure settings

## Emails
- Sender
- Recipient
- Timestamp
- Sunject line

## Music
- Artist
- Album
- Song length

## Files
- Date created
- Last modified
- File type

Streaming services like **Spotify** rely heavily on metadata to recommend artists, genres, and songs.

---
## Example: EXIF Photo Metadata
Most photos contain **EXIF metadata**, which includes:
- Date/time of photo  
- GPS coordinates  
- Camera details  
- Editing history

Helpful for:
Photographers
Digital forensics
Organization

Important for privacy:  
You can **remove EXIF data** before sharing photos.
